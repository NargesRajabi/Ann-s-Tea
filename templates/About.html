This is Ann's Tea

It all started in 2008 with one store on Queen St., Toronto, bonding with people over our passion for tea.

Fast-forward over a decade later,

we’ve connected with millions of tea lovers from coast to coast—online and IRL—through thousands of signature blends.

We’re still early on our journey, and we hope you’ll tag along as we continue to push the boundaries of what tea can be.

Together, let’s bring more people into the wonderful world of tea, one cup at a time
